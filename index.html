Crafted with Obsession, Served with Warmth

BrewHaven is a micro–roastery and coffee bar in Pune focusing on ethically sourced beans from Indian estates in Chikmagalur and Coorg. Every batch is roasted in-house in small lots for clarity and sweetness in the cup.

Our baristas brew using precise recipes and filtered water so you taste origin character, not bitterness. Whether you prefer a rich hazelnut latte or a clean cold brew, each drink is prepared to specialty standards.

Order online for doorstep delivery, or contact us for curated coffee boxes for offices, events, and gifting.
